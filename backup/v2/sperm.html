Mind-Controlled Spermatozoa

Mind-controlled spermatozoa is a project conceived and designed by interdisicplinary artist Ani Liu as an expression of female empowerment - A women controlling the movement of sperm, something inheretly and symbolically male, with her mind through a brain-computer interface.

As Ani's research assistant, I engineered the system to control the movement of sperm with Alpha brainwave via OpenBCI, a brain computer interface. The directional motility of sperm is gained through a phenomena known as galvanotaxis, where cells migrate towards a specific charge in an electric field. OpenBCI reads the electrical signals from the user's brain, and these signals are translated into commands for a microcontroller to change the direction of an electric field.
